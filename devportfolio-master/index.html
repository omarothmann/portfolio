Healthcare Management System Using Blockchain Technology

In this project, we are going solve numerous problems that currently occurred in the healthcare industry that are related with appointment system & security of data. As we can observe, many healthcare organization, clinics, or hospital are still using the traditional way to held appointments with patients. This traditional way of booking by phone or walk in booking are time consuming and not efficient. Additionally, our sensitive data shared and stored into the clinic & healthcare organization sometimes are not secured to maintain its integrity & confidentiality. To overcome such issues, we came out with a solution which is Healthcare Management System Using Blockchain Technology, or simply called as HMS.
